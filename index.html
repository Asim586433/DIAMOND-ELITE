    Forged in the Dirt

    Diamond Elite was born in 2018 on a dusty high-school field in Texas. Our founder, a former college pitcher, got tired of watching his players use gear that fell apart mid-season.

    We aren't a massive corporate conglomerate. We’re a team of former players, coaches, and die-hard fans who believe that great gear shouldn't cost a fortune. We cut out the middleman, work directly with the best leather tanneries and wood mills, and sell directly to you.

    Every bat, glove, and ball we sell is tested by real players in real games. If it doesn't perform, we don't sell it. Welcome to the lineup.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I size a baseball glove? +
    Measure from the tip of your index finger to the base of your palm. For infielders, we typically recommend 11.25" to 11.75". For outfielders, 12" to 12.75".
    What is your return policy? +
    You have 30 days from delivery to return any unused gear in its original packaging. Used gloves can be returned within 14 days if they show minimal wear.
    Do you offer team discounts? +
    Yes! If you are buying for a travel team or league, email us at teams@diamondelite.com for a custom quote.
    How long does shipping take? +
    Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Expedited 2-Day and Overnight options are available at checkout. Orders over $75 ship free!
